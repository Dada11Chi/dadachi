flow sessions, Shamanic Journeys, Telegram Group & Impulses

The heart of Chi Tribe is a meditation I developed with Spirit. Over many years. Again and again. Until it knew me and I knew it.

It does not lead you away from your thoughts. It opens the field behind them – wider, quieter, boundless. What remains is stillness. A spaciousness that feels like coming home.

From April to November, every Wednesday at 8 pm Swiss time for 30 minutes.

From December to March, every second Sunday at 11 am Swiss time for one hour.

All sessions are recorded. You won't miss a thing.

As a Chi Tribe Global member, please note that the live sessions are held in German – recordings with English subtitles are available on YouTube.

Every first Wednesday of the month you are invited to the Community Journey – a one-hour shamanic journey I offer for Chi Tribe.

In these sessions we come together to receive energy and insights from the Otherworld.

They help us to realign, to release and to reconnect – with ourselves and the greater whole.

It is a time to rest, to recharge and to remember – the deeper currents that carry us as individuals and as a community.

The Chi Tribe Telegram group is our living thread between sessions.

No feed. No algorithm. No pressure to perform.

Every Monday my voice is waiting for you there – a voice message to accompany you into the week.

A thought, an image, an energy. Your start to the week, with connection.

The recordings of all sessions and voice messages from me are there for you, helping you to integrate what you have experienced.

A place where people share what they are living and carry one another, without anyone having to play a role.

Earth’s first
aura school

The Aura School offers a clear and structured path to deepen your self-awareness. As you move through the seven degrees — from the Seed to the Forest — you begin to understand how your energy, emotions, thoughts, and deeper soul patterns interact. You learn to recognize what’s truly yours, what you’ve taken on from others, and how to shift into greater alignment with your own essence.

Each level unfolds over 63 days, giving you time to absorb and integrate. When a level is complete, you receive a certificate and the opportunity to continue, if it feels right.

This journey helps you build inner clarity, emotional balance, and trust in your intuition. Each level deepens your connection to yourself and your life, at a pace that supports true integration. A steady path to living with more presence and self-awareness.
